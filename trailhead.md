 What is Trailhead?

Trailhead is Salesforce's free online learning platform where you can learn Salesforce through hands-on exercises and earn badges.

 How to Log in

1. Open [Salesforce Trailhead](https://trailhead.salesforce.com?utm_source=chatgpt.com)
2. Click Log In
3. Sign in using:

   * Salesforce account (Developer Org)
   * Google
   * LinkedIn
   * Apple


After signup then follow salesforce admin trailhead
